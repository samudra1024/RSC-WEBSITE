Step Up

Step Up Event, an annual tradition by RSC (RNSIT Sports Club) and the RNSIT Physical Education Department, signifies more than just a run—it's a testament to community, health, and environmental awareness. In its fifth year, Step Up 5.0, held on June 11, 2023, took participants on a journey beyond the track. Themed around tree planting, the event highlighted the vital role of trees in climate action, biodiversity conservation, and soil preservation. Last year's Step Up 4.0 centered on "You Run, We Plant." Every registration led to a new sapling taking root—a tangible commitment to our planet's well-being. This theme resonated with participants, reinforcing the power of collective action. Join us in our ongoing journey, where each stride fuels sustainability and progress. Step Up Event is an invitation to move forward—together.

Agones

Welcome to Agones, the ultimate inter-departmental sports event at RNSIT! Agones is not just a competition; it's a celebration of sportsmanship, teamwork, and the indomitable human spirit. With a wide array of sports and events ranging from athletics to badminton, basketball to football, cricket to CrossFit, and table tennis to throw ball, Agones brings together diverse departments in a thrilling battle for glory. Organized by the RNSIT Sports Club, this annual extravaganza showcases the competitive essence of our college, where teams unite, champions emerge, and memories are made. Join us for a rendezvous with athleticism, camaraderie, and excitement like never before – because Agones is where departments unite, and champions rise
